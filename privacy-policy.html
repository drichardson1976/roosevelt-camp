Privacy Policy

Roosevelt Girls Basketball Day Camp 2026

Last updated: February 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register for Roosevelt Girls Basketball Day Camp, we collect the following information:

  • Parent/Guardian information: Name, email address, phone number, profile photo
  • Camper information: Name, age, grade, school, profile photo, medical notes, t-shirt size
  • Emergency contact information: Name, phone number, relationship to camper, photo
  • Account credentials: Email and password (passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing and are never stored in readable form)
  • Login activity: Login method (Google or email/password), last login timestamp

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Camp registration and management: Processing registrations, managing camp rosters, and organizing sessions
  • Safety and emergency response: Identifying campers and parents during drop-off and pick-up, contacting emergency contacts when needed
  • Communication: Sending registration confirmations, camp updates, schedule changes, and important announcements
  • Account management: Enabling login, password resets, and account recovery

3. Who Has Access to Your Information

Access to personal information is limited to:

  • Camp administrators: Camp staff who manage registrations, schedules, and day-of operations
  • Camp counselors: Counselors can see camper names, photos, and schedules for their assigned sessions (they cannot see parent contact details, emergency contacts, or account information)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the camp registration system:

  • Supabase (database hosting) — stores registration data securely in the cloud
  • Netlify (website hosting) — hosts the camp website and serverless functions
  • Google Sign-In (authentication) — optional login method; we receive only your name and email from Google
  • Resend (email delivery) — sends registration confirmations, password resets, and camp communications
  • Twilio (SMS delivery) — sends verification codes for account recovery

Each of these services has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connections)
  • Database access is restricted to authorized camp administrators
  • Serverless functions use secure environment variables for API keys

6. Children's Privacy

Roosevelt Girls Basketball Day Camp collects information about children (campers) only with the consent of their parent or legal guardian. Parents provide all camper information during the registration process.

We collect only the information necessary to operate camp safely: camper name, age, grade, school, photo (for identification during drop-off/pick-up), medical notes (for safety), and t-shirt size.

Camper photos are used solely for identification by camp staff and counselors. They are not shared publicly or used for any other purpose.

7. Your Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you and your children
  • Correct any inaccurate information through your parent dashboard
  • Delete your account and all associated data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent for data collection at any time (note: this may affect your camp registration)

8. Data Retention

We retain your information for the duration of the camp season (summer 2026). After the camp season ends, registration data will be retained for up to 12 months for administrative purposes (e.g., refund processing, returning camper records) and then deleted.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify registered users by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to exercise your data rights, please contact us:

Email: rhsdaycamp@gmail.com
