The Vision

Every girl deserves a place
to love basketball

Roosevelt Girls Basketball Day Camp is a two-week summer camp for girls who have completed 3rd through 8th grade. We focus on fundamentals, teamwork, and confidence — not just winning.

The camp runs August 17–28, 2026 at Magnuson Community Center in Seattle, with morning and afternoon sessions led by high school basketball players who serve as counselors and role models.

Camp capacity is 30 campers per session, with 1 counselor for every 5 campers. Counselors are paid $80 per session as independent contractors.

10 days, 6 hours/day — City of Seattle Parks & Recreation

$5,929

This is the single biggest fixed cost — paid regardless of enrollment

Counselor Pay

$80/session per counselor — scales with enrollment (1 per 5 campers)

Variable

$16 per camper-session (marginal cost) — only grows as enrollment grows
