The Vision

More than a camp —
a mission

A two-week day camp for girls (3rd–8th grade) at Magnuson Community Center, August 17–28, 2026. Morning and afternoon sessions led by high school varsity players.

🤝

Build Community

Girls from different elementary and middle schools meet, make friends, and build relationships that carry into high school.

💼

Jobs for Teen Athletes

Counselors earn above-market wages doing something they love — coaching basketball and giving back to their community.

🏀

Fund RHS Basketball

50% of profits go to the Roosevelt Varsity Girls Basketball program — helping cover food, travel, uniforms, and equipment.

🎯

Build Skills

Drills, scrimmages, and real coaching in small pods (5:1 ratio) so every girl gets better — whatever her starting level.

👨‍👩‍👧

A Camp Parents Can Trust

Give your daughter a summer she actually looks forward to — instead of sitting around the house. Active, social, supervised, and fun.

📈

Learn to Run a Business

Turn an idea into a profitable company — LLC formation, budgeting, hiring, marketing, and operations. Real entrepreneurship, not a class project.

Revenue Model

Session Price$60 per half-day
Full-Week Discount10% off
Multi-Week Discount15% off
Sessions per Day2 (AM + PM)
Camp Days10 weekdays

Camp capacity is 30 campers per session, with 1 counselor for every 5 campers. Counselors are paid $80 per session as independent contractors.

Cost Structure

Where the money goes

Gym Rental — Magnuson Community Center

10 days, 6 hours/day — City of Seattle Parks & Recreation

$5,929

This is the single biggest fixed cost — paid regardless of enrollment

Counselor Pay

$80/session per counselor — scales with enrollment (1 per 5 campers)

Variable

$16 per camper-session (marginal cost) — only grows as enrollment grows

Technology & Operations

Website hosting, database, email service, domain

~$0

All on free tiers — custom-built website eliminates SaaS subscription costs

Break-even: ~146 camper-sessions — after that, 50% of every dollar of profit goes to RHS Girls Basketball

With 20 session-slots over 10 days and up to 30 campers per session, the total addressable market is 600 camper-sessions
