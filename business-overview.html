The Vision

Every girl deserves a place
to love basketball

Roosevelt Girls Basketball Day Camp is a two-week summer camp for girls who have completed 3rd through 8th grade. We focus on fundamentals, teamwork, and confidence — not just winning.

The camp runs August 17–28, 2026 at Magnuson Community Center in Seattle, with morning and afternoon sessions led by high school basketball players who serve as counselors and role models.

Camp capacity is 30 campers per session, with 1 counselor for every 5 campers. Counselors are paid $80 per session as independent contractors.

Cost Structure

Where the money goes

Gym Rental — Magnuson Community Center

10 days, 6 hours/day — City of Seattle Parks & Recreation

$5,929

This is the single biggest fixed cost — paid regardless of enrollment

Counselor Pay

$80/session per counselor — scales with enrollment (1 per 5 campers)

Variable

$16 per camper-session (marginal cost) — only grows as enrollment grows

Technology & Operations

Website hosting, database, email service, domain

~$0

All on free tiers — custom-built website eliminates SaaS subscription costs

Break-even: ~146 camper-sessions — after that, every session sold is profit

With 20 session-slots over 10 days and up to 30 campers per session, the total addressable market is 600 camper-sessions

The Journey

From idea to real business

Late January 2026

First line of code

Dad and daughter start building the camp website using Claude AI. Login page, parent signup, and admin dashboard built in a single evening.

Early February

Full platform working

Parent onboarding, counselor applications, session calendar, payment tracking, pod assignments — over 200 updates shipped in 2 weeks.

February 28

LLC formed

Roosevelt Girls Basketball LLC officially filed with the State of Washington. This is a real company.

March 9

WA State certificate + IRS EIN

Certificate of Formation received from the Secretary of State. Federal Employer Identification Number obtained from the IRS.

March 11

Credit card payments go live

Stripe integration added — parents can now pay with a credit card directly on the website, in addition to Venmo.

March 19

Gym permit approved

Seattle Parks & Recreation approves permit #R282920 for Magnuson Community Center — 10 days, August 17–28. Facility secured.

March – April

Counselors recruited, families registering

9 counselors signed up. Families are creating accounts, registering campers, and paying for sessions. The camp is happening.
